At Scigilian, I was effectively working as a full stack developer. It was my first experience in a professional workplace, and boy was it a good one. I considered returning here, but I ultimately decided to use my co-op program to experience different workplaces. I was given the 'Excellent' rating for my evaluation. In your face, Salad.

Frontend Intern

I've only now started my internship at Soluteo. I will be working full-time on the frontend of a web application. So far, the people are nice. Can't really say much else without damning myself later on! The interviews were fun and the questions were fair. I reserve judgement, for now.
